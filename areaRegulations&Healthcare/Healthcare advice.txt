When you are well
1. Wash your hands often with soap and water. This includes before and after eating and after going to the toilet
2. Use alcohol-based hand sanitisers when you canâ€™t use soap and water
3. Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth clean and disinfect surfaces and objects you use often
4. Increase the amount of fresh air by opening windows or changing air conditioning
5. There is no evidence that alcohol-free hand rubs are effective against viruses like COVID-19.

When you are sick
1. As we move towards living COVIDSafe, it is important that you stay at home if you feel unwell. You should also continue to practise good hygiene.
2. If you have cold or flu like symptoms you should seek medical advice and get tested for COVID-19. You should get tested even if your symptoms are mild.
3. If you have COVID-19 you must isolate and follow the directions of your local Public Health Unit.
